Frequently Asked Questions

To invest in an opportunity, navigate to the "Investment Opportunities" page, review the deal details, and click "Express Interest." Our team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss the investment process, minimum requirements, and next steps.

Your dashboard provides a complete overview of all your active investments. You can view detailed performance metrics, funding progress, and expected returns for each deal. Quarterly reports with comprehensive updates are also available in the "Portfolio Reports" section.

Return timelines vary by investment type and are specified in each deal's prospectus. Infrastructure projects typically have terms ranging from 5-8 years with potential for interim distributions. Specific payment schedules and expected return dates are outlined in your investment documentation.

All investment documents, prospectuses, legal agreements, and quarterly reports are available in the "Documents" section of your portal. You can view them online or download them for your records. Documents are organized by category for easy access.

Navigate to "My Profile" to view your account information. To update contact details, company information, or communication preferences, click "Edit Profile Information" or contact [email protected] for assistance.

Our support team is here to help! Contact us via email at [email protected], call +1 (561) 555-0100 during business hours, or schedule a call with an advisor. We typically respond to inquiries within 24 business hours.
